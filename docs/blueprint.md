# **App Name**: VogueVerse

## Core Features:

- Announcement Bar: Displays site-wide announcements.
- Product Listing and Filtering: Displays and filters products by size, color, price, and style.
- Social Proof Integration: Displays a shoppable Instagram feed to showcase user-generated content.
- Secure Checkout Process: Streamlined checkout with guest option.
- Email Capture: Sign-up form for newsletters and offers.
- Dynamic Hero Section: Large, engaging section showcasing videos or images.
- "Spin to Win" Gamification: Interactive wheel for discounts.
- Motivational Mondays Content Moderation: A tool to ensure submissions align with brand values, maintaining a positive community image. This uses AI to classify submission types (stories, images, videos), prioritize for human review, and identify potential copyright or content issues.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to evoke trust and sophistication in alignment with modern fashion trends.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECEFF1), a subtle, desaturated tone that allows products to stand out.
- Accent color: Vibrant pink (#E91E63) for calls to action and interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' sans-serif for headlines, with an elegant, fashionable feel; body text: 'Alegreya' serif for readability.
- Clean, minimalist icons related to shopping, social media, and user interaction.
- Responsive, grid-based layout that adapts to various screen sizes.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user engagement and provide visual feedback.
